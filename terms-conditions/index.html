What should travelers know before booking?

Bookings are confirmed only after full payment and written confirmation. Most tours start around 5:00 AM, and the meeting point is confirmed in writing. Refunds depend on when written cancellation notice is received. Because Mindo tours take place in cloud forest conditions, participants should be prepared for rain, mud, uneven trails, wildlife encounters, and changing weather.

Tour Terms

The sections below keep booking expectations clear before you confirm your Mindo birding plan.

1. Booking and payment

  • Preferred booking window: Please book at least 48 hours in advance whenever possible.
  • Minimum booking window: Reservations must be confirmed at least 24 hours before the tour start time. Requests inside 24 hours are subject to availability and may include a late-booking surcharge.
  • Day-before bookings: Tours booked the day before must be paid in full, in advance, by Zelle or bank transfer to be confirmed.
  • Confirmation: A booking is confirmed only after full payment is received and Mindo Bird Watching sends written confirmation.

2. Pricing and minimum participants

  • Pricing is based on a minimum of two paying participants.
  • If only one traveler books, the single traveler pays the equivalent of two participants unless we can add the traveler to another confirmed group.
  • If we are able to add a single traveler to a shared group, we will adjust the booking and refund any applicable difference.

3. Start time and meeting point

  • Most birding tours begin at 5:00 AM so the group can reach active birding areas near dawn.
  • Pickup location or meeting point is coordinated and confirmed in writing before the tour.

4. Client cancellations and refunds

  • 48 hours or more before the tour: eligible for a refund of the amount paid, minus any non-recoverable transaction or processing fees.
  • The day before the tour: eligible for a 50% refund of the tour price, minus any non-recoverable transaction or processing fees.
  • Less than 24 hours before the tour or no-show: non-refundable.
  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method whenever possible. Processing times vary by bank or payment processor.

5. Rescheduling

  • One reschedule is permitted when requested at least 48 hours before the tour start time, subject to availability.
  • Requests inside 48 hours are treated as cancellations under the cancellation policy.

6. Operator changes and cancellations

  • We may modify, delay, or cancel a tour because of weather, safety concerns, guide illness, road closures, park restrictions, or operational reasons.
  • If we cancel before the tour begins, you may choose a full refund or a reschedule, subject to availability.
  • Tours cancelled by the operator are 100% refundable. Transaction and processing fees are covered by us for operator-initiated cancellations.

7. Participant responsibilities and assumption of risk

  • Participants should inform us in advance about medical conditions, allergies, mobility limitations, or special needs.
  • Participants must follow guide instructions. Wildlife sightings are natural experiences and cannot be guaranteed.
  • Participants should dress for early morning cloud forest conditions, including rain, mud, uneven terrain, and changing temperatures.

8. Children and minors

  • Participants under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian, or provide signed consent or waiver documentation when applicable.
  • Age limits may apply depending on route, terrain, weather, and safety conditions.

Terms FAQs

Answers to common booking and policy questions.

When is my booking confirmed?

Your booking is confirmed after full payment is received and Mindo Bird Watching sends written confirmation.

Can I reschedule a tour?

One reschedule is permitted if requested at least 48 hours before the start time, subject to availability.

Are bird sightings guaranteed?

No. Birding happens in natural habitats, so sightings depend on season, weather, timing, habitat conditions, and wildlife behavior.

What should I bring?

Bring clothing for rain and mud, comfortable walking shoes, water, binoculars or camera gear, and any personal medication you may need.
