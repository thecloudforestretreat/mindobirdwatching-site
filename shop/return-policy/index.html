Shop Return Policy

All items sold in the Mindo Bird Watching shop are made to order through our print partner. Because each product is created specifically for your order, standard returns are not accepted.

Made-to-Order Products

Every item in our shop is produced individually after an order is placed. This helps reduce waste and allows us to offer a curated selection of designs inspired by the cloud forest of Mindo, Ecuador.

For this reason, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges for:

  • Incorrect size selection
  • Change of mind
  • Ordering the wrong item
  • Color preference

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, we will gladly help resolve the issue.

Please contact us within 30 days of delivery and include:

  • Your order number
  • A photo of the damaged or incorrect item
  • A brief description of the issue

Once verified, we will arrange a replacement or refund depending on the situation.

Lost Packages

If your order appears to be lost during shipping, please contact us and we will work with our print partner and the shipping carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.
