Shop Shipping Policy

All Mindo Bird Watching shop products are made to order through our print partner. This page explains production timing, estimated delivery windows, tracking, international shipments, and how to ask for help before or after ordering.

What should visitors know before using the shop?

A quick answer before you order.

The Mindo Bird Watching shop uses made-to-order fulfillment, so each product is produced after checkout and shipped through our print partner. Production usually takes a few business days, delivery time depends on your destination, and tracking is sent once the order ships.

Production and estimated shipping times

Timing depends on product type, order volume, destination, and carrier conditions.

Production time

Most apparel and accessories are produced after checkout and usually take 2 to 5 business days before shipping.

United States

Typical delivery after production is about 3 to 7 business days, depending on carrier service and destination.

Canada and Europe

Typical delivery after production is about 5 to 10 business days, depending on customs and local carriers.

Other international orders

International destinations often take about 7 to 20 business days after production, with possible customs delays.

Order tracking and support

Useful details if you already placed an order.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you should receive a tracking number by email. Tracking may take a short time to update after the carrier receives the package.

Multiple shipments

Orders with more than one product may ship separately if items are produced in different facilities or require different packaging.

International fees

Customs duties, import taxes, and local delivery fees may apply for international shipments. These charges depend on the destination country and are the customer's responsibility.

Questions

If you have questions about shipping or an existing order, contact us at mindobirdwatching@gmail.com. We usually respond within 24 hours.

Shipping policy FAQs

Short answers for common shop questions.

What should visitors know before using the Mindo Bird Watching shop?

The shop uses made-to-order fulfillment through a print partner. Production begins after checkout, shipping times vary by destination, and tracking is sent when the order ships.

How long does production usually take?

Most apparel and accessories take about 2 to 5 business days to produce before shipping. Busy production periods or specific product types may take slightly longer.

Do international orders include customs fees?

International shipments may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, or local carrier fees depending on the destination country. These charges are the customer's responsibility.

Can I ask about shipping before ordering?

Yes. Contact Mindo Bird Watching if you have a question about product options, shipping expectations, tracking, or an order issue.
