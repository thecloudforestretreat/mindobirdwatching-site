Shop Shipping Policy

All products in the Mindo Bird Watching shop are made to order through our print partner. Each item is produced after an order is placed and shipped directly to you.

Production Time

Because our products are printed individually after an order is placed, production typically takes:

  • 2–5 business days for most apparel and accessories
  • Some products may take slightly longer depending on production demand

Estimated Shipping Times

Delivery times depend on the destination and shipping carrier. Typical delivery estimates after production include:

  • United States: 3–7 business days
  • Canada: 5–10 business days
  • Europe: 5–10 business days
  • International destinations: 7–20 business days

Shipping times may vary depending on customs processing, carrier delays, or destination location.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number by email so you can monitor delivery progress.

If you do not receive a tracking update within a few days of production, please contact us.

Multiple Shipments

Orders containing multiple items may ship separately depending on the product type and production location.

International Orders

International shipments may be subject to customs duties or import taxes depending on the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer.
