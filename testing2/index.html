Quad Tour in Mindo, Ecuador

Ride an ATV through muddy forest tracks and river crossings with a guide and safety gear on one of the most fun adventure activities in Mindo. Quad tours combine off-road riding, countryside scenery, and plenty of laughs as you splash through mud and explore the green hills around town.
