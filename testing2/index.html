How ordering works

  1. Choose a product and open the Printify link.
  2. Checkout securely through the print partner.
  3. Production + shipping are handled on demand.

You will receive order and shipping emails from the print partner. If you need help choosing sizes, framing, or gifts, contact us anytime.

Shop FAQ

Clear expectations for made-to-order items.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes. Shipping availability and rates depend on the product and destination shown at checkout.
How long does it take?
Items are made to order. Expect production time plus shipping. Exact estimates appear at checkout.
Can I request a custom design?
Yes. If you want a specific bird, photo style, or a personalized label, contact us and we will tell you what is possible.
Returns and exchanges
Because items are made to order, return policies depend on the print partner and the product type. If something arrives damaged, contact us and we will help you resolve it.
