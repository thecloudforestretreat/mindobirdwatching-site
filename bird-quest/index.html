Use this page before and during your Mindo tour as a playful field guide. Each card teaches one species with photos, bird calls, kid-friendly clues, and an interactive spotted checklist for the current visit.

Frequently asked questions

Can kids use Mindo Bird Quest before a tour?

Yes. Kid Mode turns each species into a simpler field mission with one easy clue, one ID tip, and one listening challenge.

Does marking a bird as spotted send data to Mindo Bird Watching?

No. The MVP keeps spotted birds only during the current page visit and does not send the checklist to Mindo Bird Watching.

Which tour is best for the birds in this quest?

Many starter birds fit a half-day route near Mindo town, while quetzals, manakins, and specialty species usually need a full-day or custom private route.

Want to turn this quest into a real birding route?

Tell us your dates, birding level, target species, photography goals, and available time. We will suggest a realistic Mindo route for your group.
