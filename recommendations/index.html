Where should birders stay in Mindo?

Prioritize calm lodging, early breakfast options, reliable access, and a location that works with pre-dawn or early morning birding routes.

Should I book lodging before choosing a birding route?

If target birds matter, ask your guide first. Your route, start time, and transportation can affect which area is easiest.
