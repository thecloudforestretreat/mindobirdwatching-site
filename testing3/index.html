Chocolate Tour in Mindo, Ecuador

Mindo is located in Ecuador’s famous cacao region, home to some of the world’s finest chocolate. This guided chocolate tour introduces visitors to the full journey of cacao — from tropical cacao trees growing in the cloud forest to the roasting, grinding, and tasting that produces rich Ecuadorian chocolate.

During this relaxed experience you will learn how cacao pods are harvested, how the beans are fermented and dried, and how traditional chocolate is crafted. Guests also enjoy tasting different stages of cacao and finished chocolate while discovering why Ecuador is globally known for its premium cacao.

What you will experience

A hands-on introduction to Ecuadorian cacao and chocolate.

Cacao trees and pods

Learn how cacao grows in the tropical climate around Mindo and see real cacao pods before they are harvested.

Chocolate making process

Your guide explains fermentation, drying, roasting, and grinding — the key steps that transform cacao beans into chocolate.

Chocolate tasting

Taste several stages of cacao and finished chocolate while learning how flavor profiles develop.
