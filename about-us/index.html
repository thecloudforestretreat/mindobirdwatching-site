About Us

At Mindo Bird Watching, we are passionate about sharing the breathtaking beauty of Ecuador’s cloud forest and its incredible birdlife. With years of experience as a birdwatching guide, I specialize in helping visitors discover the vibrant world of toucans, hummingbirds, tanagers, and countless other species that call Mindo home.

Our tours are designed to provide a unique and immersive experience, whether you are a seasoned birder or a first-time nature enthusiast. With expert guidance, personalized attention, and access to some of the best birding spots in the region, we ensure every moment is unforgettable.

Join us to explore the wonders of Mindo and create memories surrounded by the songs and colors of nature.
