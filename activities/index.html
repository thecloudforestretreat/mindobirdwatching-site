Discover Mindo's Best Adventures

At Mindo Bird Watching, our passion starts with the incredible birdlife of the cloud forest, home to colorful toucans, countless hummingbirds, and the iconic Andean Cock-of-the-Rock.

But there's so much more to experience here. From adrenaline-filled adventures like ziplining, rafting, and canyoning to relaxing nature tours like butterflies, orchids, and coffee and chocolate tastings, Mindo offers something unforgettable for every kind of traveler.

Explore all our activities below and plan your perfect mix of birdwatching, adventure, and discovery in Mindo, Ecuador.
