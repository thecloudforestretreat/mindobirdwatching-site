Policy Details

Clear sections for website visitors, tour inquiries, and booking communication.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mindo Bird Watching. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website or use our services, including booking birdwatching tours, subscribing to updates, or contacting us for information.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information when you interact with our website or services.

  • Personal information: name, email address, phone number, and information you provide when contacting us, subscribing, or booking a tour.
  • Booking details: tour dates, group size, birding interests, photography goals, and special requests related to your reservation.
  • Usage data: browser type, device information, pages visited, and similar website analytics data collected through cookies or analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage tour inquiries or bookings.
  • To communicate with you about reservations, questions, or trip planning.
  • To send updates or newsletters if you choose to receive them.
  • To improve website content, usability, and visitor support.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with trusted service providers only when needed to fulfill your request, such as booking tools, payment processors, email platforms, analytics providers, or communication services. We do not share information with unauthorized third parties for independent marketing.

5. Data Retention

We retain booking and contact information only as long as needed to provide services, answer follow-up questions, maintain reasonable business records, and meet legal or operational obligations.

You may request deletion of your information by contacting mindobirdwatching@gmail.com.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as booking tools, payment processors, analytics platforms, email providers, or messaging services. These services may process information under their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, update, or deletion of your personal information. You may also unsubscribe from newsletters or marketing messages at any time.

8. Security

We use reasonable precautions to protect information submitted through our website. No internet transmission or storage method is completely secure, so we encourage visitors to avoid sending sensitive information through standard website forms or messaging platforms.

9. Contact Us
