How to think about birdwatching spots in Mindo

A strong birding location can underperform if visited at the wrong time. Good route design connects the right place with the right activity window.

Think in trip goals

If you are broadly interested in cloud forest birding, your route can stay flexible. If you care more about particular groups, the location strategy becomes more selective.

Think beyond DIY lists
