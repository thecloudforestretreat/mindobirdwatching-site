Helpful notes

Come ready for height, motion, and secure footwear.

Wear comfortable clothes and secure footwear, and avoid loose items unless they can be stored safely before the swing. The activity is short but exciting, and it is best enjoyed by travelers who are comfortable with heights and want a quick, highly visual thrill.

FAQs

Quick answers to help decide whether this activity fits your trip.

How long does the Double Extreme Swing take?

The experience is usually short. Most of the time is for preparation, safety, and waiting; the swing moment itself is fast and intense.

Can you combine it with birdwatching?

Yes. It works well as an add-on after a birding outing, between short activities, or as part of a mixed Mindo activity day.

Is it good for families?

It can be good for families with older children who meet the operator's age, height, or safety rules and feel comfortable with heights.

What should I bring?

Wear comfortable clothes, secure footwear, and avoid loose items. If you want photos, check first how phones or cameras are handled.
