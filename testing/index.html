Why Cabañas Las Nubes stands out in Tena, Ecuador

Cabañas Las Nubes is one of the most appealing lodging options for travelers searching for a nature retreat in Tena, Ecuador. Its cloud forest setting, peaceful atmosphere, and access to trails and river scenery make it a natural fit for birdwatching, slower travel, and nature-oriented stays in the Amazon region. For visitors looking for an Ecuador lodge experience that feels more connected to the landscape than a standard hotel, Las Nubes offers a strong balance of comfort, biodiversity, and direct contact with the outdoors.

Cabañas Las Nubes provides a quiet and comfortable base for travelers exploring the Amazon region of Ecuador. The lodge blends simple comfort with direct access to forest trails, river landscapes, and birdwatching opportunities, making it ideal for nature-focused stays.

Location and directions
