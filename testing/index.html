Hummingbirds of Mindo

Mindo is one of the easiest places in the world to see hummingbirds up close, with dozens of species moving through gardens, feeders, and forest edges every day.

Book a Tour See Tours

The quick answer

  • Best time: morning and late afternoon
  • Easiest viewing: feeders and gardens
  • Best approach: stay still and patient
