Tour Terms

Simple terms to keep bookings clear and fair.

1

Booking and Payment

  • Preferred booking window: Please book at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Minimum booking window: Reservations must be confirmed at least 24 hours before start time. Requests inside 24 hours are subject to availability and may incur a late fee.
  • Day-before bookings: Tours booked the day before must be paid in full, in advance, via Zelle or bank transfer to be confirmed.
  • Payment requirement: All tours must be paid in full in advance. A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of payment and written confirmation from us.
2

Pricing and Minimum Participants

  • Minimum group size: Pricing is based on a minimum of two (2) paying participants.
  • Single traveler policy: If only one person is booked, the single traveler pays the equivalent of two participants unless we can place you in another group. If we can add you to a group, we will adjust and refund any applicable difference.
3

Start Time and Meeting Point

  • Standard start time: Tours begin at 5:00 AM.
  • Pickup/meeting: Pickup location or meeting point is pre-coordinated and confirmed in writing.
4

Cancellations and Refunds (Client)

Notify us in writing to cancel. Refunds are based on when we receive your written notice:

  • 48 hours or more before the tour: 100% refund.
  • The day before the tour: 50% refund.
  • Less than 24 hours before the tour or No-Show: Non-refundable.
  • Refund method and timing: Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary by bank/processor.
5

Rescheduling

  • One-time reschedule is permitted if requested at least 48 hours before start time, subject to availability.
  • Requests inside 48 hours are treated as cancellations under Section 4.
6

Changes and Cancellations (Operator)

  • We may modify, delay, or cancel a tour due to weather, safety, guide illness, road closures, park restrictions, or operational reasons.

  • Tours canceled by the operator are 100% refundable.
7
