All products in the Mindo Bird Watching shop are made to order through our print partner. Each item is produced after checkout and shipped directly to the customer.

Where We Currently Ship

Our Google Merchant Center shop setup currently supports shipping within the United States. If international shipping becomes available through our shop setup later, we will update this page and the checkout details.

Made-to-Order Production

Products are printed individually after an order is placed. This helps us offer Mindo-inspired designs without holding warehouse stock.

  • Handling / production time: usually 2-5 business days
  • Fulfillment days: Monday-Friday
  • Order cutoff: 2:00 PM Eastern Time

Transit Time

After production, standard carrier transit within the United States is usually 3-7 business days. Combined production and transit time is estimated at 5-12 business days.

Carrier delays, holidays, weather, incorrect addresses, or remote delivery locations may affect final delivery timing.

Shipping Cost

United States standard shipping is currently set as a flat $6.99 USD shipping charge. Final checkout details should always be reviewed before purchase.

Tracking

When carrier tracking is available, tracking details are sent after the order ships. Tracking can take a short time to update after the label is created.

Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Orders

If an item arrives damaged, defective, misprinted, or incorrect, contact us within 30 days with photos so we can review it for replacement or refund under our return policy.
