Interview: Get To Know Chicho

Key questions about his experience, guiding style, and love for Mindo.

What inspired you to become a birdwatching guide?
I’m inspired by the chance to observe so many wonders and colors in Mindo every day.
How long have you been guiding birdwatching tours in Mindo?
I have guided for more than six years, and every season I still learn something new from the forest.
What is your favorite bird and why?
I love the Andean Cock-of-the-Rock and the Highland Toucan because of their colors, calls, and presence in the forest.
What is the best time of year to bird in Mindo?
Mindo can be visited year-round, but activity changes with rain, fruiting trees, breeding season, and time of day.
Do you have a favorite trail or birding spot?
The best route depends on your target birds, level, weather, and available time, so I prefer to plan each outing personally.
