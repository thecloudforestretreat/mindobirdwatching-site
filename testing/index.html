Roca Mía in Mindo, Ecuador

Roca Mía is a quiet riverside lodge near Mindo for travelers who want cloud forest, rustic cabins, and a stronger connection to birdwatching habitat.

Its clearest nature value is the private reserve setting and access associated with the Andean Cock-of-the-Rock, one of the most iconic birds of Ecuador's cloud forests.
