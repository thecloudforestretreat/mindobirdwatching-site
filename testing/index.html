About Us

At Mindo Bird Watching, we share the breathtaking beauty of Ecuador's cloud forest and the birdlife that makes Mindo one of the most rewarding places to bird in South America.

With years of field experience as a birdwatching guide, our focus is helping visitors discover toucans, hummingbirds, tanagers, the Andean Cock-of-the-Rock, and many other species through patient, personalized guiding.

Our tours are built for experienced birders, photographers, families, and first-time nature travelers. We combine local route knowledge, flexible pacing, and practical planning so each guest can enjoy the forest with confidence.

About Mindo Bird Watching FAQs

Answer-first details about our guiding style, planning support, and who our tours are for.

Who is Mindo Bird Watching?

Mindo Bird Watching is a local guiding and tour planning service focused on birdwatching experiences in Mindo's cloud forest.

What makes the guiding style different?

We personalize each outing around guest goals, target species, weather, photography needs, available time, and current bird activity.

Can beginners join?

Yes. Tours are welcoming for first-time birders and nature travelers, while still being useful for experienced birders and photographers.

How should I start planning?

Share your dates, group size, birding level, target species, and whether you are coming from Quito or staying in Mindo.
