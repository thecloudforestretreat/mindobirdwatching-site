Recent sightings FAQs

Useful answers before you plan around eBird records or local reports.

Are recent sightings guaranteed?

No. A recent sighting means a bird was reported nearby, but weather, fruiting trees, time of day, access, and observer effort can change your chances.

Do I need a guide?

A guide is strongly recommended if you have target birds, limited time, photography goals, or want to compare several routes efficiently.

What should I send?

Send your dates, number of birding days, birding level, camera/binoculars, and any species you especially hope to see.

Can this help with a day trip?

Yes. A recent review can help decide whether to focus on gardens, forest roads, reserves, or a private dawn-to-afternoon route from Quito.
