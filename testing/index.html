Return Policy for Made-to-Order Products

Our shop products are printed and fulfilled after an order is placed. Because each item is made to order, general returns and exchanges are not accepted, but we do review requests when an item arrives defective, damaged, misprinted, or incorrect.

This page explains what qualifies for support, what information to send, and how to contact Mindo Bird Watching before returning any product.

Shop items are made after purchase, so returns are limited to product or fulfillment issues.

Made-to-Order Items

This policy helps reduce waste while keeping small-batch birding designs available.

Why returns are limited

Every product in the Mindo Bird Watching shop is produced by our print fulfillment partner after purchase. Because each item is made specifically for the order, we cannot accept returns for wrong size, wrong color, change of mind, buyer remorse, or accidental duplicate orders.

When we can review an issue

If your order arrives defective, damaged, misprinted, or different from what you ordered, contact us within 30 days of delivery. We will review the issue and, when approved, help arrange a replacement or refund.

How to Report a Defective, Damaged, Misprinted, or Incorrect Product

Send enough information for us to review the issue before any product is returned.

What to include

  • Your order number or purchase email.
  • Clear photos of the full product.
  • Close-up photos showing the defect, damage, misprint, or incorrect item.
  • A photo of the packaging if the shipment arrived damaged.

Contact us first

Please do not send a product back before contacting us. We need to review the issue first and confirm the correct next step.

FAQs About Shop Returns

Concise answers for common return and replacement questions.

Does Mindo Bird Watching accept returns?

We accept return or replacement requests only for defective, damaged, misprinted, or incorrect products. General returns are not accepted because shop products are made to order.

Does Mindo Bird Watching accept exchanges?

No. Exchanges are not accepted. If you need a different size, color, or style, please place a new order.

How long do I have to report a defective product?

Please contact us within 30 days of delivery and include your order number, purchase email, and clear photos of the issue.

Should I send the product back first?

No. Contact us first so we can review the issue and confirm whether a return, replacement, refund, or another next step applies.
