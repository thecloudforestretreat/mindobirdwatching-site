Birds commonly photographed or targeted

No sighting or photo is guaranteed; these are realistic possibilities by route and conditions.

Toucans and barbets
Toucan Barbet, Plate-billed Mountain Toucan, Yellow-throated Toucan, Choco Toucan, Pale-mandibled Aracari, and Crimson-rumped Toucanet.
Hummingbirds
White-booted Racket-tail, Violet-tailed Sylph, and other hummingbirds depending on feeder access and route choice.
Lek and forest birds
Andean Cock-of-the-Rock, Golden-headed Quetzal, Scaled Fruiteater, Crested Guan, Bronze-winged Parrot, and Rufous Motmot.
Tanagers
Blue-winged Mountain Tanager, mixed flocks, and colorful cloud forest tanagers when light and movement cooperate.
Water and valley birds
Torrent Duck and route-specific birds when the selected route includes suitable river habitat.
